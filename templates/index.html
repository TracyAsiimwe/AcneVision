Pipeline

How it works

01
Face Detection
OpenCV Haar Cascade locates and crops the facial region, isolating only the skin area for analysis.
02
CNN Classification
Fine-tuned MobileNetV2 classifies acne severity into one of four clinical levels with a confidence score.
03
Feature Mapping
Computer vision analysis annotates lesion locations. Grad-CAM heatmap and medical report generated instantly.
Tips for the best results
Use a clear, well-lit frontal face photo
Natural or indoor lighting works best
Avoid heavy makeup, filters, or coverings
Face should be centered and clearly visible
Selfie distance gives the most accurate results
